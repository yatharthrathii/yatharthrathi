Projects

BlissBazar

I led the development of BlissBazar, an e-commerce platform using React for frontend and Strapi with Render for backend management. It offers a user-friendly experience with features like a powerful search bar, categorized listings, and a diverse product section. The platform ensures swift product retrieval and display with a responsive design, catering to various devices. Notably, it includes comprehensive cart functionality, showcasing my full-stack development skills and commitment to innovation in e-commerce.

EmpowerHerHaven

EmpowerHerHaven UI is dedicated to empowering women at risk of homelessness by showcasing their talents through handcrafted books, perfumes, and skincare products. Every purchase contributes to breaking the cycle of homelessness, providing opportunities for financial independence and a renewed sense of purpose. By offering a platform for these resilient individuals to sell their creations, EmpowerHerHaven fosters success stories of triumph over adversity, creating a tangible impact on the lives of women in need.
